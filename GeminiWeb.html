Practical Demos & Prompts

This is where theory meets practice. Below are concrete examples and prompts you can use directly in your favorite Google apps. Select a tab to see the demos for that tool, and use the copy button to grab any prompt you'd like to try.

Scenario 1: Instant Analysis

Quickly understand your data without writing a single formula.

Analyze the data in this sheet and identify the top 3 products by revenue. Create a bar chart to visualize these results.

Scenario 2: Automated Planning

Create a structured plan from a simple request.

Create a project plan for a new product launch. Include columns for task, owner, due date, and status. Populate it with 5 example tasks.

Scenario 1: From Idea to Deck

Generate an entire presentation outline in seconds.

Create a 5-slide presentation on our Q3 company goals. The first slide should be a title slide. The following slides should cover 'Sales Growth,' 'Product Innovation,' and 'Customer Satisfaction.'

Scenario 2: Content & Image Help

Refine a slide and find visuals automatically.

Summarize this slide into three bullet points and generate a professional image of a team collaborating.

Scenario 1: Effortless Email Drafting

Write professional emails without the effort.

Draft a follow-up email to a client after our meeting. Thank them, summarize the key points (Project X timeline, budget), and suggest a follow-up call next week.

Scenario 2: The Instant Summary

Catch up on long conversations with a single click.

Action: In a long email thread, open the Gemini side panel and click the pre-populated "Summarize this thread" button.

No prompt needed! Gemini automatically suggests this highly useful action for you.

How to Get Started Today

Ready to dive in? Adopting Gemini is easy. Here are a few simple steps to begin integrating AI into your workflow. The key is to experiment and see what works for you.

1.

Look for the Gemini Icon

You'll see the ‚ú® icon at the top of your Google Workspace apps. That's your entry point to AI assistance.

2.

Start Simple

Don't overthink it. Ask Gemini to summarize an email or help you rephrase a single sentence to be more professional.

3.

Be Specific with Prompts

The more detail and context you provide in your request (your "prompt"), the better and more relevant the result will be.

4.

Experiment!

There's no right or wrong way to use it. Play around, try different prompts, and discover how Gemini can best support your unique role.
